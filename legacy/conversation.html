What is Teflon?

10:42 AM • Delivered

Teflon is a brand name for a synthetic chemical called polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE). It's best known for its use in non-stick coatings for cookware, but it's also used in clothing, industrial applications, and medical devices due to its high heat resistance and low friction.

Is Teflon harmful for health?

10:44 AM • Delivered

Teflon itself is generally considered non-toxic. However, health concerns are primarily related to PFOA (perfluorooctanoic acid), a chemical previously used to manufacture Teflon. Modern Teflon products are PFOA-free.

"Concerns also arise if non-stick pans are overheated (above 500°F/260°C), which can cause the coating to break down and release fumes."
